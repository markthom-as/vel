# vel.md

Vel is a **personal cognitive operating system** designed to extend human executive function.

Vel continuously maintains awareness of:

- goals
- projects
- commitments
- ideas
- conversations
- time allocation

Vel assists by:

- capturing thoughts
- remembering context
- resurfacing relevant information
- aligning actions with goals
- coordinating automated tools

Vel operates across devices including:

- phone
- watch
- workstation
- servers

Vel is designed to run **locally first**, with optional distributed compute.

The long-term vision is that Vel becomes **self-hosting** — a system capable of managing and evolving its own infrastructure.

Vel is not simply an assistant.

Vel is a **collaborative executive system** that helps its user think, plan, and act more effectively over time.
