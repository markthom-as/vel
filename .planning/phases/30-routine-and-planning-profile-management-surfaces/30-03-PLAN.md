# 30-03 Plan

## Slice

Expose summary-first planning-profile management in shipped surfaces over the canonical backend-owned profile.

## Why this slice third

After the inspect/mutate seams are real, Vel can let operators manage the planning profile from shipped surfaces while keeping `Now`, `Threads`, and `Settings` thin and aligned over the same truth.

## Outcomes

- `Settings` can inspect and manage routine blocks and bounded planning constraints through typed backend seams
- `Now` and `Threads` stay summary-first over the same planning-profile posture
- no client-owned planning heuristics or parallel planner state
