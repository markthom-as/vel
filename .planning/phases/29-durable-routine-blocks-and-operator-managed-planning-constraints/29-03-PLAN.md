# 29-03 Plan

## Slice

Use durable routine blocks in day-plan shaping and expose summary-first management in shipped surfaces.

## Why this slice third

After the records are durable, Vel can improve same-day planning quality and let operators inspect/manage the posture through `Now`, `Threads`, and `Settings` without creating shell-owned planner logic.

## Outcomes

- `day_plan` consumes durable routine blocks and bounded planning constraints
- `reflow` stays on the same planning substrate
- shipped surfaces expose summary-first routine-backed planning posture and bounded management affordances
