# 33-01 Plan

## Slice

Publish the approved day-plan/reflow application contract and lifecycle over commitment scheduling.

## Why this slice first

Before any surface can review or apply same-day planning changes, Vel needs one explicit contract for proposal state, approval semantics, and the relationship between bounded planning output and the canonical commitment scheduling seam.

## Outcomes

- shared lifecycle contract for approved same-day schedule application
- transport/API vocabulary for pending, applied, failed, and dismissed continuity
- explicit reuse of existing supervised proposal/application patterns instead of planner-specific exceptions

## Likely Touch Points

- `crates/vel-core/src/`
- `crates/vel-api-types/src/`
- `docs/cognitive-agent-architecture/architecture/`
- `config/schemas/`
- `config/examples/`

## Verification

- parse checks for checked-in schema/example assets
- doc truth checks against existing day-plan, reflow, planning-profile, and supervised-action contracts
