# 33-03 Plan

## Slice

Expose pending and applied day-plan/reflow continuity across `Now`, `Threads`, CLI, and Apple without creating a second planner.

## Why this slice third

After the backend apply path exists, shipped surfaces need a compact, truthful way to show pending review pressure and applied scheduling outcomes while keeping planner authority in the backend.

## Outcomes

- compact summary-first continuity for same-day schedule proposals and outcomes
- `Threads` as the durable follow-through lane for longer schedule disagreement or failed application
- cross-surface parity over one backend-owned continuity model
