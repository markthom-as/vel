# 32-01 Plan

## Slice

Publish the approved planning-profile application contract and proposal lifecycle transitions.

## Why this slice first

Before any surface can review or apply staged planning-profile proposals, Vel needs one explicit contract for proposal state, approval semantics, and the relationship between staged conversational edits and the canonical mutation seam.

## Outcomes

- shared lifecycle contract for approved planning-profile proposal application
- transport/API vocabulary for proposal state and applied outcome continuity
- explicit reuse of existing supervised proposal/application patterns instead of planning-profile-specific exceptions
