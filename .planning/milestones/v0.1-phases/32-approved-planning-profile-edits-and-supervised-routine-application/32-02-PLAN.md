# 32-02 Plan

## Slice

Implement backend approval and application of staged planning-profile proposals through the canonical mutation seam.

## Why this slice second

Once the lifecycle contract is explicit, the most important missing behavior is the real backend bridge from `staged` planning-profile proposal to approved or applied durable profile change.

## Outcomes

- backend-owned approval/application path for staged planning-profile proposals
- reuse of canonical planning-profile validation and persistence instead of bespoke conversational writes
- explicit failure and applied-outcome recording in durable continuity
