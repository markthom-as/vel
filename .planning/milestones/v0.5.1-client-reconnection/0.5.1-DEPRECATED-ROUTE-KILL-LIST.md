## Disposition Vocabulary

- `rewrite` — replace with canonical client transport usage inside the frozen `Now` / `Threads` / `System` model
- `quarantine` — keep temporarily with named rationale and explicit removal or re-entry trigger
- `delete` — remove in `v0.5.1`

Unnamed temporary shims are forbidden.
