# 70-01 Plan

## Objective

Reconnect `Threads` and introduce `/system` as the single authoritative structural surface.

## Work

1. Rebind `Threads` to canonical interaction truth and allow only object-scoped invocation.
2. Ship `/system` as a single-route surface with stable internal sections for domain, capabilities, and configuration.
3. Expose invocation, explain, and state visibility only through canonical backend outputs.

## Acceptance

- `Threads` no longer depends on stale client semantics
- object-less workflow invocation is impossible
- `/system` exists as one route, not a mini-app cluster
- no builders/editors/simulators are introduced
