# 69-01 Plan

## Objective

Rebind `Now` to canonical task/calendar/availability truth and keep it as a thin execution surface.

## Work

1. Replace stale `Now` data sources with canonical read models only.
2. Render tasks and calendar commitments as adjacent canonical sections rather than a merged ranked feed.
3. Reconnect direct triage mutations through canonical `WriteIntent`.
4. Keep `defer` or `snooze` semantics restricted to explicit due/date mutation only.
5. Absorb `Inbox` behavior into `Now` triage/query flows without reviving a separate `Inbox` surface.

## Acceptance

- `Now` renders canonical operational truth only
- all `Now` mutations are canonical writes
- tasks and calendar commitments remain adjacent canonical sections with no synthetic cross-section ordering
- `defer` and `snooze` do not become abstract client-side semantics
- no client-side ranking, inferred urgency, hidden sorting logic, or recent-change ghost state remains
