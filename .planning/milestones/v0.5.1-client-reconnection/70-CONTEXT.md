# Phase 70 Context

## Goal

Reconnect `Threads` and ship the minimal authoritative `/system` surface.

## Boundary

This phase reconnects contextual and structural surfaces. It must not widen into a workflow builder, a new settings app, or route sprawl.

## Required Outputs

- canonical `Threads` read flow
- object-scoped manual workflow invocation in allowed surfaces only
- `/system` as one route with stable internal sections
- fixed first-pass `System` sections and a pre-frozen action allow-list for structural areas
- no builders, editors, or client-side workflow simulation
