# 74-01 Plan

## Objective

Rework `Now` into the intended operator surface without reintroducing client-side semantic invention.

This phase may not change the general `Now` layout unless the layout/design definition has already been explicitly approved.

## Work

1. Redesign `Now` layout around adjacent canonical sections and clearer action hierarchy.
2. Tighten direct triage flows so canonical mutations feel immediate.
3. Remove obvious churn and latency from the active `Now` path.
4. Preserve `Focus` as the execution locus rather than letting it collapse into another peer list.
5. Treat the approved `Now` layout/design contract as authoritative rather than improvising structure during implementation.

## Acceptance

- `Now` feels denser, clearer, and faster
- tasks and calendar commitments remain adjacent canonical sections
- triage remains canonical and direct
- no synthetic merged ranking feed or local prioritization is introduced
- calendar remains a today-first constraint surface, not a browseable future surface
- general `Now` layout remains aligned to the approved design contract
