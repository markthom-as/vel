# v0.3 Milestone Audit

## Outcome

`v0.3` is closable.

The milestone goal was to turn the canonical `Now` surface and client mesh into one Rust-owned cross-platform product lane. That goal is met closely enough to close the milestone without hidden carryover.

## What v0.3 Delivered

- the canonical `Now` contract is durable repo authority and reconciled against the full local source contract
- shared Rust-owned DTOs and service seams now own header, status row, context line, nudge bars, task lane, docked input, thread routing hints, mesh posture, and governed `Now` config
- web now embodies the compact canonical `Now` surface over that shared transport
- iPhone/iPad, Mac, and reduced watch now follow the same compact `Now` contract closely enough to claim parity
- client-mesh and sync posture are shared product concepts rather than platform-specific shell guesswork
